The Lentor story

A proven precinct, on its most keenly-priced land.

Lentor has quietly become one of Singapore's most sought-after new private estates — successive launches, consistently strong take-up, and resale already climbing past launch prices. Lentor Gardens Residences is the latest chapter: a 499-home development by Kingsford, minutes on foot from Lentor MRT, wrapped in parks and reputable schools — and built on the lowest-cost land of the entire Lentor cycle.
